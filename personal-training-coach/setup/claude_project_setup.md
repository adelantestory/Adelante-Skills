# Setup Guide — Claude.ai Project (Mobile Friendly)

*Use this guide if you primarily use Claude on your phone, tablet, or browser.*

---

## Requirements

- Claude.ai account (Free, Pro, or Team plan)
- 15 minutes to complete setup

---

## Step 1 — Fill In Your Client Profile

Open `CLIENT_PROFILE.md` in any text editor and fill in every section. Be thorough — especially the health flags and equipment sections. The quality of your program depends on this information.

Save the file when done.

---

## Step 2 — Create A New Project

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
2. Click **Projects** in the left sidebar
3. Click **New Project**
4. Name it something like "My Training Coach" or your name + "Training"

---

## Step 3 — Set The Project Instructions

1. Inside your new project, click the **project settings** or **edit instructions** option
2. Open `COACH_INSTRUCTIONS.md` in a text editor
3. Select all the text (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A) and copy it
4. Paste it into the project instructions field
5. Save

---

## Step 4 — Upload Your Files

In the project, upload these three files:
- Your completed `CLIENT_PROFILE.md`
- `SKILL.md`
- `SESSION_LOG.md`

These files give Claude the context it needs for every session.

---

## Step 5 — Start Your First Session

Open the project and type:

> "I'm ready to start. Please review my profile and design my training program."

Claude will review your CLIENT_PROFILE.md, ask any clarifying questions, establish your baselines, and build your program. This first session takes 20–30 minutes.

---

## Ongoing Use

**Every morning:** Open the project and post your weight.
> "April 20 — 181 lbs"

**After training:** Post your session results.
> "Monday Push A — Bench: 4×8×175lb, Incline DB: 4×10×45lb, Duration: 75 min, Energy: 8/10"

**Every four weeks:** Claude will prompt you to do a rebaseline. Take your measurements and retest your lifts.

**Updating your log:** After each session, Claude will provide a formatted SESSION_LOG entry. Copy it and paste it into your SESSION_LOG.md file, then re-upload the updated file to the project.

---

## Tips For Mobile Use

- Keep session result posts short and structured — the format above works well on mobile
- If Claude's response is too long, ask: "Give me just the key points for today"
- Use voice-to-text on your phone to post session results hands-free in the gym
- Star or bookmark the project for quick access from your home screen

---

## Troubleshooting

**Claude doesn't seem to remember previous sessions:** Make sure you're opening the same Project, not starting a new conversation outside the project.

**Claude asks for information already in your profile:** Re-upload your CLIENT_PROFILE.md — it may not be reading the latest version.

**The program doesn't feel right:** Tell Claude directly. "This program isn't working for me because [reason]." Claude will adjust.
