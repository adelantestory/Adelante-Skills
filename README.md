# Adelante Skills

A collection of AI coaching frameworks built for Claude. Each skill is a self-contained folder you can drop into a Claude.ai Project or Claude Cowork Project to get a personalized, ongoing coaching experience — no technical setup required.

---

## What Is This?

These frameworks turn Claude into a specialized coach for different areas of your life. Unlike a one-time prompt or a static plan, each skill is designed for **ongoing conversations** that build context over time. Claude learns your situation, tracks your progress, and adapts its guidance as you grow.

Every skill works on two platforms:

| Platform | Best For | Requirements |
|---|---|---|
| **Claude.ai Project** | Mobile and browser users | Any Claude plan |
| **Claude Cowork Project** | Desktop users who want full automation | Pro or Max plan + Claude Desktop |

---

## Available Skills

### 🏋️ [Personal Training Coach](./personal-training-coach/)
A goal-agnostic fitness and nutrition coaching framework. Works for any goal — muscle building, body recomposition, weight loss, strength, endurance, or general health. Adapts to your equipment, schedule, physical limitations, and lifestyle. Tracks sessions, weight trend, and strength progression. Rebaselines every four weeks.

**Good for:** Anyone who wants a structured, personalized training and nutrition program with ongoing coaching and accountability.

## How To Use A Skill

Each skill folder contains everything you need:

1. **Download or clone** the skill folder you want
2. **Fill in your personal profile** template with your goals and background
3. **Follow the setup guide** for Claude.ai Project or Claude Cowork
4. **Start your first session** — Claude reviews your profile and builds your personalized plan

Full setup instructions are in each skill's `README.md` and `setup/` folder.

---

## Philosophy

These skills were built from real coaching engagements — not theoretical frameworks. The methodology in each skill was developed through practical application, refined based on what actually worked, and generalized so it serves a wide range of people and situations.

The goal is not to replace human coaches, doctors, or financial advisors. It is to give everyone access to a knowledgeable, personalized, ongoing coaching conversation — regardless of budget or location.
