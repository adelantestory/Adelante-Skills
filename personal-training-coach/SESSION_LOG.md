## How To Use This File

**Daily:** Post your morning weight in the Weight Trend section.
**After training:** Post your session results in the Training Sessions section.
**Every 4 weeks:** Post your rebaseline measurements and strength tests in the Rebaselines section.

Keep entries in reverse chronological order — most recent at the top of each section.
